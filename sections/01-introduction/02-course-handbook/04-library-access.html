Accessing the Online Library

As a sub-section of Key Resources, this guide walks you through accessing the digital library system.

Step-by-Step Login

  1. Navigate to the library portal
  2. Click "Student Login"
  3. Enter your student ID and password
  4. Use the search bar to find course materials by title or ISBN

Your library access is active for the duration of your enrollment. E-books can be downloaded for offline reading through the library app.
