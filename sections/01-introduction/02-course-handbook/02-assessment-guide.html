Late Submission Policy

Late submissions will incur a penalty of 5% per day, up to a maximum of 5 days. After 5 days, submissions will not be accepted without prior arrangement.

Academic Integrity

All work submitted must be your own. Plagiarism and other forms of academic dishonesty will be dealt with according to institutional policy.
