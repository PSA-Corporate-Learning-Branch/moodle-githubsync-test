Essential Safety Rules

Always follow these critical safety rules in the workplace:

  1. Wear appropriate PPE at all times in designated areas
  2. Report all hazards immediately to your supervisor
  3. Know the location of emergency exits, fire extinguishers, and first aid kits
  4. Never operate equipment without proper training and authorization
  5. Keep work areas clean, organized, and free of obstructions
  6. Follow all posted safety signs and instructions
